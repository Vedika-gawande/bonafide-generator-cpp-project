Automated Bona-Fide Certificate Generator

User Help Guide

Introduction

Welcome to the Automated Bona-Fide Certificate Generator! This system allows students to request bona-fide certificates securely using blockchain technology.

How to Use

1. Student Module

  • Click on the Student button on the homepage.
  • Enter your Enrollment Number and Phone Number.
  • Click Verify OTP, enter the received OTP, and verify.
  • Fill out the registration form with required details.
  • Submit your request for verification.
  • Once approved, your certificate will be sent to your registered phone number.

2. Staff Module

  • Click on the Staff button and log in using credentials.
  • View student requests and verify the provided reasons.
  • If valid, forward to the Principal for approval.
  • The Principal reviews, signs, and sends the certificate to the student.

Key Features

  • Blockchain Security: Ensures authenticity and prevents tampering.
  • OTP-Based Verification: Secure login and request submission.
  • Automated Workflow: Reduces manual processing.
  • Instant Certificate Issuance: Quick and efficient service.
